My Mission

I build my business one client at a time. I have made service a trademark of my business and enjoy long term relationships with my clients. My team includes a transaction coordinator, an assistant and escrow managers. We possess a fervor for real estate that has benefited my clients and their home-buying aspirations.

My Vision

I have established a reputation for integrity, professionalism and devoted service. I look forward to working with you to help you satisfy your real estate needs and investment goals.
I have completed several tax courses along with exchange courses. Many tax-deferred exchanges have been done for clients since the 1986 Tax Reform Act. I have a separate property management division, Comprehensive Property Services that helps clients manage property if they do not wish to be involved in the daily operation of rentals.

My Value

I have a long association with many professionals who will help prepare a home or apartment building for the market. I can pass along discounts on new carpet, carpet cleaning and painting to clients who need these services. Due to the large volume of property that I list and sell, my clients receive priority from outside professionals when we need to get a property "market ready."

Personal Background

I am a transplanted Californian and have lived in the Bay area for almost 40 years. I love living here and enjoy the weather, the diversity and the incredible flow and exchange of ideas. Through Rotary, I have become involved in working with Rotaplast, an organization devoted to repairing cleft lip and cleft palates in children and adults in developing countries. I have worked on funding missions since 2006, currently involved with the Sabor de Valle festival in San Jose into a larger event that supports Rotaplast. I have a son that lives in the Willow Glen area and he keeps me technically savvy since that is his field of expertise. I love to travel to experience other cultures.

Professional Achievements

I have sold residential and investment properties all over the Bay Area. I have consistently been in the top 1% of real estate sales agents for the last ten years. I am a charter member of Contempo Realty’s Platinum Club. I received the Centurion Award from Century 21 for over 10 million in sales in 1997. I received the Centurion Award for over 12 million in real estate sales in 1998. My sales production for 1999 was over 21 million and I received the Grand Centurion Award. My sales production for 2000 was 27 million in sales and I received the Grand Centurion Award. I’ve been the number one listing agent, number one buyer’s agent and Top Producer for the years 1999 and 2000. I have been a member of Coldwell Banker’s International President’s Premier for the year 2001, 2002, and 2003. International President’s Premier is awarded to agents who produce a minimum of $550,000 in Closed Adjusted Gross Commission Income. I was a panelist at the Superstar Retreat in 1997. I was a panelist on the Mike Ferry Superstar Panel for the Santa Clara County Association of Realtors Tradeshow in September 1999.
